## Naming Convention
* Level of authority *add or Edit* Kung ano man yun 
### Example:
    ** Level1AddBusinesses.php**
* dat may levels tas yung level palagi mauuna sa name. sa Index lang nababago kasi yung parang home yun.
* index[Level ng authority]
** Example:**
    * indexAdmin.php or
    * indexLevel1.php
